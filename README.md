# SwiftRoute Logistics Dashboard

A business-focused logistics analytics project built in **Power BI** to study delivery performance across **orders, hubs, drivers, and vehicles**. The dashboard turns operational data into a clear decision-making report that highlights service quality, delay patterns, capacity use, and fleet health.

---

## Business Context

Logistics operations depend on speed, reliability, and coordination. When deliveries are delayed, hubs become overloaded, driver performance varies, or vehicles break down, the entire service chain is affected.

This project was created to answer the most important operational questions:
- How many orders are being completed?
- Are deliveries reaching customers on time?
- Which hubs are handling the workload efficiently?
- Which drivers are performing well and which ones are contributing to delays?
- Which vehicles are reliable, and which ones create risk?

The dashboard presents those answers in a simple, visual, and business-friendly way.

---

## What the Dashboard Shows

### Overview Page
The main page provides a high-level performance snapshot with:
- Total Orders.
- On-Time Delivery Rate.
- Customer Satisfaction Score.
- Average Delivery Time.
- Hub performance view.
- Driver performance view.
- Vehicle utilization view.

### Hubs Page
This page focuses on operational efficiency at hub level and shows:
- Total number of hubs.
- Orders processed versus hub capacity.
- Hub performance ranking.
- Hub order processing time.

### Drivers Page
This page evaluates driver performance and shows:
- Total number of drivers.
- Experience versus rating.
- Drivers with the most delays.
- Driver profile summary.
- Monthly trend of orders.

### Vehicles Page
This page studies fleet health and usage patterns and shows:
- Total number of vehicles.
- Active vehicles.
- Total orders by vehicle model.
- Vehicle age versus breakdowns.
- Breakdown by vehicle code.
- Breakdown by vehicle model.
- Orders by vehicle type.

## Business Insights

This dashboard is designed to help management interpret logistics performance, not just view charts.

- **Total Orders** helps measure overall workload and demand movement.
- **Month-over-Month change** shows whether performance is improving or declining.
- **On-Time Delivery Rate** is the clearest indicator of service reliability.
- **Customer Satisfaction Score** links operational performance with customer experience.
- **Average Delivery Time** helps identify speed issues in the delivery chain.
- **Hub Capacity vs Orders Processed** reveals overload risk and balance issues.
- **Hub Processing Time** highlights locations where orders are taking too long to move forward.
- **Driver Experience vs Rating** helps understand whether skill and tenure are reflected in performance.
- **Drivers with Most Delays** can be targeted for coaching, route review, or workload balancing.
- **Vehicle Age vs Breakdown** helps identify maintenance risk and fleet replacement needs.
- **Orders by Vehicle Type and Model** show which vehicles are most heavily used and where fleet optimization is needed.
